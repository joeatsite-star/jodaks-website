# App Store Listing

## App Name

Sara — Cart Offers & Rewards

## Tagline

Custom teasers that build bigger carts to win your reward

## Description

Sara watches your customers' carts in real time and shows them a custom message the moment they reach a spending threshold you choose.

### How it works

You set a cart value goal for your reward, then write the message customers see once they reach it. You can also add one or more teaser messages along the way, each tied to a percentage of your goal. For example, you might show "You're halfway there!" at 50 percent of the threshold, and a closer reminder at 75 percent, building anticipation as the customer's cart grows. Once they reach your full goal, Sara automatically swaps the teaser for your final reward message, shown right on the storefront in real time. You can create multiple separate offers at different cart values, each with its own teasers and reward message, giving you full control over how shoppers are encouraged at every stage of their cart.

### Why merchants use Sara

- Encourages bigger orders by giving shoppers a clear, visible reason to add one more item
- Fully customisable messaging and colours to match your store's branding
- Set up in minutes, no coding or theme editing required
- Works automatically once installed, no ongoing manual work needed

### Built for simplicity

Sara is designed to do one thing well: turn cart value into a clear, motivating moment for your customers. No complicated dashboards, no confusing settings, just a clean way to encourage bigger carts.

Sara is free to install and use. More advanced features, including automatic discount codes and sales analytics, are coming soon.
